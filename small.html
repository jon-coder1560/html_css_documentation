The small Element

The small element represents side-comments and small print — often legal disclaimers or attribution text. It renders text smaller than the surrounding text.

Example Code:

This line has a <small>small print</small> note at the end.

The Result:

This line has a small print note at the end.

W3C: <small>

Design tip

Use sparingly — extremely small text can harm readability on mobile devices.
