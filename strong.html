The strong Element

The strong element indicates that its contents are of strong importance, seriousness, or urgency. It is semantic — meaning is conveyed to assistive technologies. Visually it appears bold by default.

Example Code:

Please read the <strong>important notice</strong> before proceeding.

The Result:

Please read the important notice before proceeding.

W3C: <strong>

Accessibility

Because <strong> conveys importance semantically, screen readers will emphasize it appropriately.
