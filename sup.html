The sup Element

The sup element renders text as superscript — typically used for footnote markers, exponents, and ordinal indicators.

Example Code:

X<sup>2</sup> represents X squared (X²).

The Result:

X2 represents X squared (X²).

W3C: <sup>

Rendering notes

Superscript and subscript are purely presentational; they don’t affect semantic meaning beyond presentation.
