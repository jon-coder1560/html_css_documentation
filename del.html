The del Element

The del element represents content that has been removed from a document. Typically rendered with a strikethrough. Use it to show deletions or revision history.

Example Code:

This sentence contained <del>an old word</del> that is now gone.

The Result:

This sentence contained an old word that is now gone.

W3C: <del>

Revision extra

You can add a cite or datetime attribute to express why/when content was removed.
