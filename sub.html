The sub Element

The sub element renders text as subscript — typically used for chemical formulas and mathematical expressions.

Example Code:

H<sub>2</sub>O is written with a subscript for the 2.

The Result:

H2O is written with a subscript for the 2.

W3C: <sub>

Use cases

Common in formulas, citations, and chemical notation.
